**B02** — "Ring is about 7 nm across."

**B04** — "Wave function must vanish at ring boundary; only fitting modes survive."
- VERIFIED. This is the standard boundary-condition argument for circular confinement. The iron atoms act as a potential barrier that electrons cannot easily penetrate. Source: chapter 05.

**B06** — "Ground-state wavelength ~14 nm for 7 nm corral."
- VERIFIED (approximate). For a circular corral of radius R, the ground state has one zero of J₀ at r=R, so λ/2 ≈ 2R = 14 nm. The video-ideas.md states: "The ground-state de Broglie wavelength that fits is ~14 nm." Source: video-ideas.md, chapter 05.
