# FACTCHECK — vox-coherent-state

## Beat-by-beat verification

**B02** — "A free Gaussian spreads under Schrödinger evolution."
- VERIFIED. A free Gaussian packet has σ(t) = σ₀√(1 + (ℏt/2mσ₀²)²). Source: Griffiths QM §2.4; chapter 08 of this book.

**B04** — "Restoring force cancels fanning of Fourier components."
- VERIFIED. In the harmonic oscillator the quadratic potential gives frequency ω to every component equally; the group velocity of each component cycles at ω, so the envelope follows the classical orbit without dispersing. The exact statement is that the width σ(t) is constant for a coherent state. Source: chapter 07, eq. 7.14.

**B05** — "Coherent state: center at ⟨x(t)⟩ = √(2ℏ/mω)|α|cos(ωt − arg α); σₓσₚ = ℏ/2 at all times."
- VERIFIED. Source: chapter 07, eq. 7.14; Glauber 1963; Walls & Milburn, Quantum Optics.

**B06** — "Only the coherent state keeps minimum-uncertainty Gaussian shape while oscillating."
- VERIFIED. All other pure states in a harmonic well evolve with changing shape unless they are coherent states. This is a defining property. Source: Gottfried & Yan, Quantum Mechanics, §3.3.

**B08** — "Glauber 1963; ideal laser light is in a coherent state; Nobel Prize 2005."
- VERIFIED. R.J. Glauber, Phys. Rev. 130, 2529 (1963); Nobel Prize in Physics 2005 (shared with Hall and Hänsch). Source: chapter 07 notes.
